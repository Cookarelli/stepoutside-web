Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

Core tracking works without creating an account.

Does StepOutside use my location all the time?

No. Location is used for walk-related features like tracking distance and nearby suggestions.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed in your app store account settings.

How do I restore purchases?

Open the app, go to Pro/Manage Pro, and tap Restore Purchases.

How do I contact support?

Email support@stepoutside.app.
