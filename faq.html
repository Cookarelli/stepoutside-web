Frequently asked questions

Is Step Outside free?

Yes. You can start building an outdoor habit for free, with Pro available for deeper tracking and premium features.

What's included in Pro?

Pro includes unlimited saved walks, advanced progress tracking, premium insights, Golden Hour analytics, and early access to select new features.

Can I use it indoors?

Yes. Step Outside is built for outdoor time, but timer fallback and indoor backup walks can help you keep the habit going when needed.

Does Step Outside track GPS walks?

Yes. Step Outside can track GPS walks, and it also includes fallback options when location is unavailable.

Can I walk with friends?

Yes. You can walk with friends anytime, and Step Outside is continuing to build friend-focused features for shared motivation.

Are challenges free?

Some challenge experiences are planned to be available for free, with Pro adding deeper tracking and premium challenge tools over time.

Can Pro users create challenges?

Custom challenge creation is planned as a Pro feature. It should be treated as coming soon unless you see it available inside the app.

Is Android coming?

Yes, Android support is in progress. A Google Play test link may be available before the public Android launch.

Can businesses sponsor challenges?

Yes. Businesses can reach out about future sponsored challenges, local rewards, and community wellness campaigns.

How does Step Outside support mental wellness?

Step Outside encourages short walks, calmer routines, reflections, and meaningful time away from screens. It is a wellness tool, not medical advice.

Is Step Outside a fitness app?

Not really. Step Outside is built as a calm daily reset app focused on short walks, consistency, and mental clarity rather than workouts or performance.

Does Step Outside use my location all the time?

No. Location is used for walk-related features like tracking distance, nearby reset suggestions, and local Golden Hour timing.

What happens after I finish a walk?

You will see a completion screen, a short reflection step, and then a simple share-or-return flow.

Can I use the app if I deny location?

Yes. Some features are reduced, but the app still works with timer fallback and ZIP-based route suggestions.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed in your App Store account settings.

How do I restore purchases?

Open the app, go to Pro, and tap Restore Purchases.

How do I contact support?

Email support@stepoutside.app.
