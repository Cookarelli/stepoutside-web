Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-18

StepOutside (“we,” “us,” “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information we collect

  • App activity data: walk sessions, duration, distance, streak and app settings.
  • Approximate/precise location: used during walk-related features (tracking, nearby suggestions, weather windows).
  • Purchase metadata: subscription/entitlement status via billing providers and service partners.
  • Support communications: emails/messages you send us.

2) How we use data

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Generate stats and habit insights
  • Show nearby suggestions and weather timing
  • Manage subscriptions and restore purchases
  • Respond to support requests

3) Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with service providers needed to operate the app (e.g., analytics, payments, infrastructure), subject to contractual protections.

4) Data retention

We keep data only as needed for app functionality, legal compliance, and support. You may request deletion by contacting us.

5) Your choices

  • Location permissions can be managed in device settings.
  • Notifications can be managed in app/settings.
  • You may request account/data deletion by emailing support@stepoutside.app.

6) Children

StepOutside is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

7) Security

We use reasonable safeguards but no system is 100% secure.

8) Changes

We may update this policy. Material updates will be reflected with a new effective date.

9) Contact

Email: support@stepoutside.app
