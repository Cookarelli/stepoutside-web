Request data deletion

We respect your right to delete your data.

How to request deletion

  1. Email support@stepoutside.app with subject: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the email/account identifier you used in the app (if any).
  3. We may ask for verification to protect your privacy.

We aim to complete valid deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal obligations.
