Features

A richer reason to get outside every day.

Step Outside brings habit-building, exploration, social encouragement, and mindful outdoor time into one calm app experience.

Smart Walk Tracking

Track outdoor walks with GPS, fallback timers, active walk recovery, and a flow designed for consistency instead of pressure.

Golden Hour Rewards

Turn sunrise and sunset walks into special moments with Golden Hour bonuses, streaks, and seasonal reasons to step outside.

Friends

Add friends, follow each other's outdoor progress, and make the daily habit feel shared without turning it into a noisy feed.

Challenges

Join daily, weekly, seasonal, and team challenges that reward showing up for real time outside.

Personal Stats

See outside minutes, walk streaks, Golden Hour activity, challenge progress, and the small wins that keep the habit alive.

Discover Places

Find nearby reset routes, local outdoor ideas, indoor backup walks, and ZIP-based suggestions when location is unavailable.

Capture the Moment

Save meaningful walks, note what shifted, and capture the small outdoor moments that make the habit personal.

Mental Wellness

Use short walks, reflections, Daily Spark reminders, and calmer routines to disconnect from noise and return with more clarity.

Founder note

Why I built Step Outside

I built Step Outside during one of the hardest seasons of my life.

Every morning I forced myself to take a walk.

Those walks slowly changed everything.

Less stress. Better perspective. Better habits.

Step Outside exists to help people experience that same reset — one walk at a time.

Steven Cook
