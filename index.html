A short walk still counts.

Track a walk with GPS or timer fallback, keep a streak going, and build a habit that feels supportive instead of heavy.

Golden Hours make the day feel different.

Catch sunrise and sunset moments, earn Golden Hour bonuses, and build a rhythm around the most restorative parts of the day.

Reflect before you move on.

After each walk, take a quiet moment to notice what shifted. Step Outside includes a simple post-walk reflection flow designed to support calm and consistency.

Find a nearby reset.

Browse short route suggestions, indoor backup walks, and ZIP-based fallbacks when location is not available.

Keep it simple.

Daily Spark reminders, streak support, and low-pressure encouragement help you come back without turning your life into a performance dashboard.

Built to feel calm, not competitive.

Step Outside is designed for consistency and mental clarity. The goal is not intensity. The goal is to help you step out, reset, and come back steadier.
