Founder note

Why I built Step Outside

I built Step Outside during one of the hardest seasons of my life.

Every morning I forced myself to take a walk.

Those walks slowly changed everything.

Less stress. Better perspective. Better habits.

Step Outside exists to help people experience that same reset — one walk at a time.

Steven Cook Founder, Step Outside
